Cristian's Overnight Oats

How to make my overnight oats!

The picture is not what my oats looks like, just don't have a picture

Here are the ingredients you will need:

  1. steel cut oats
  2. protein powder
  3. granola
  4. chia seeds
  5. almond milk
  6. mixed berries

All you have to do is put all ingredients into a mason jar and let it sit overnight.
