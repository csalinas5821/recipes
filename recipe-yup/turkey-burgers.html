Cristian's Turkey Burgers

How to make my turkey burgers!

Here are the ingredients you will need:

  • trader joes turkey burger patties
  • any bun/bread
  • red onion
  • tomato
  • avocado
  • ketchup/mayo

Cooking it is quite simple as well, here are the steps:

  1. heat a pan with olive oil on medium high heat
  2. while pan heats, chops onion and tomato
  3. put turkey burger patties on pan, cook oneside for 6-8 minutes
  4. season with preferred seasonings
  5. flip, cook for another 6-8 minutes
  6. toast bread and prepare a bed for the burgers in the bread

This recipe is dairy free
