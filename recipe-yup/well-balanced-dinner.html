Well Balanced Dinner

How to make a well balanced dinner!

I like to have at least one protein, one carb, and one veggies

here are the ingredients I like to use:

Proteins:

  • chicken breast
  • ground beef
  • salmon

Carbs:

  • potatoes/sweet potatoes
  • whole wheat pasta
  • rice

Veggies:

  • brocolli
  • carrots
  • zucchini
  • cauliflower
  • green beans

How I cook my meal:

  1. I typically start with my carb because they take the longest unless pasta then its last, get rice cooking or boil/oven potatoes
  2. then I'll start my protein, oil and heat a pan
  3. season meat once its on pan
  4. while meat cooks on one side, begin prepping veggies
  5. depending on veggie, stove cook it or boil them
  6. flip meats, reseason
  7. serve once all are complete
